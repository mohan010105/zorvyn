Food
Transport
Entertainment
Utilities
Shopping

Budget amount
Total spent
Remaining budget
Progress bar.

Select category

Save budget.

Total spent per category
Remaining budget

Remaining Budget = Budget Limit − Category Expenses

90% spent → Danger
100% spent → Budget exceeded.

"Warning: You have used 75% of your Food budget."

"Alert: You exceeded your Entertainment budget."
